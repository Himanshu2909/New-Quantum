What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools a unique global challenge. Teams of students aged 9 to 19 design, construct and race miniature Formula 1 cars of the future, powered by a compressed air cylinder. The challenge inspires teams to use IT to learn about physics, aerodynamics, designing, manufacturing, graphics, marketing, teamwork, media skills, and apply them in a competitive yet practical and exciting way.

The competition takes place at various levels- regionally and nationally and finally, the best teams win a place at the F1 in Schools World Finals. Teams raise sponsorship to fund researches, travel, manufacturing and prepare marketing strategies to promote sponsors and F1 in Schools.

QUANTUM is born

Six students from Grade XI come together to work towards a common cause: building the fastest F1 car prototype.

Marketing activities initiated

In the marketing front, Quantum aims to build a reputable brand identity, one that the sponsors would proudly associate with. Our unique approach to marketing, based on Simon Sinek’s ‘Start With Why’ enabled us to come up with a marketing framework at par with industry standards wherein a number of activities ranging from tech sessions to digital media campaigns were taken into consideration.

Let's Quanvo!

Quanvo is a talk show started by our team in which we interact with experts and professionals from diverse industries, relevant to our campaign, on themes like Leadership, Entrepreneurship, Innovation and Technology. Streaming on various platforms like Spotify, Anchor, Apple podcasts and YouTube enabled us to reach to a greater number of people.

Tachyon on the Tracks

After months of work on designing, manufacturing and assembling, the pinnacle of our team; Tachyon, was made. It is central to our team identity as it is a personification of swiftness, innovation and precision. For the purposes of achieving perfection, we developed two algorithms which reduced our time exponentially and provide highly accurate optimizations.

QUANTUM: National Champions

After about a year of relentless hard work, Team Quantum Racing emerged as the National Champion of F1 in Schools India Open Finals along with Best Pit Display Award, Most Innovative Thinking Award, Best Enterprise Portfolio award and 3 other nominations, making us the most awarded Indian team ever.
